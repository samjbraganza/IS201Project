My name is Sam Braganza, I was born in india and grew up in England.
I moved to england when I was six years old.
Growing up, I wanted to be in a rock band and tour the world.
I was in a rock band for three years and played lead guitar at different venues to raise money for charities.
I was first introduced to finance when I was a volunteer for my church in India.
There I had a mentor who had a few successful businesses. I was inspired by the freedom and flexibility that his businesses gave him.
He was helping others, had time for his family and had time to pursue his hobbies.
He encouraged me to pursue finance when I returned home.
I decided to study at BYU because of the great finance program at the marriott school of business.
Here, I was introduced to the world of investment banking through the Investment Banking Association.
This put me on a path to pursue investment banking today.
